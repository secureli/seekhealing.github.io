Our mission depends upon the generosity and support of donors like you. A financial or in-kind contribution of any amount can make all the difference in helping a seeker struggling with opioid addiction find a pathway toward the authentic connection experiences that create lasting, life-long healing.

SeekHealing is a Virginia not-for-profit corporation and a public charity under section 501(c)3 of the Internal Revenue Code. Your donation to SeekHealing may be tax-deductible.

Volunteer

All of us can be seekers, searching for connection, for meaning, and for growth in our lives. All of us can support other seekers in their own journeys.

You can volunteer to help SeekHealing in its mission to facilitate the healing journey of seekers struggling with heroin or opioid addiction.

Whatever your background, wherever you are in live, reach out today to find out how you can help a fellow seeker through the SeekHealing project.
