About SeekHealing

A detox aftercare model that unlocks the neurochemical benefits of authentic connection experiences. This non-profit organization provides equal-opportunity programs that use principles of connection & community to empower sustainable healing after detox from opioid & heroin addiction.

For more information about SeekHealing

Founded in 2016, SeekHealing is a 501(c)3 charity and post-detox recovery model that challenges mainstream stigmas about opioid and heroin addiction. Our programs turn the idea of standard rehab upside down by providing affordable services that prioritize authentic connection and harm reduction.

Our programs are designed for the 6-12 months after detox that are most critical to sustainable recovery and mental health integration. The methodology is focused to enhance the neuroplasticity of participants through: intentional experiences of authentic social & spiritual connection; rewarding the practice of learning new things; and facilitating a major change to their daily environment.

Program features include peer-based therapy, personalized community & career transitions, and guidance through intentional experiences of authentic connection. Using leading research in psychology and neuroscience, these experiences are designed to create a neurochemical context for healing and habit-change in the brain.
