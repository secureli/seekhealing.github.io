About SeekHealing

SeekHealing is a detox aftercare model that unlocks the neurochemical benefits of authentic connection experiences. This non-profit organization provides equal-opportunity programs that use principles of connection & community to facilitate sustainable healing after detox from opioid & heroin addiction.
